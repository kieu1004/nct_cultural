NCT 127

Neo Culture Technology

NCT 127 is the second sub-unit of the South Korean boy band NCT operating mainly in Seoul , founded and managed by SM Entertainment. The group officially debuted on July 7, 2016 with the single "Fire Truck" with an initial lineup of seven members: Taeil, Taeyong (leader), Yuta ,Jaehyun , Winwin, Mark and Haechan. The group's debut mini-album NCT #127 was a huge hit right from the time of its debut and brought the group the Best New Group award at major music awards in Korea in 2016. Two members Doyoung and Johnny joined the group in December 2016, not long before the group released the mini album Limitless in early 2017. Jungwoo, the last member, was introduced in September 2018 before the group's release. Released their first studio album, Regular-Irregular (2018).
